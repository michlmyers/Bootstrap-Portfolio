    About Me

    Growing up outside of Philadelphia I was always interested in music. After experimenting with multiple instruments I eventually settled on the drumset. I began to write music and play in local bands before being accepted to Berklee College of Music, in Boston, and majoring in their Music Business program.

    After graduating from Berklee I moved to New York to land a job on the business side of the music industry, working with performing rights organizations, radio, and record labels. During this time I have continued to remain interested in my creative side by writing music with local bands. Web development is a natural expansion of my creative and business experience for the next stage in my life. I currently reside in Jersey City with my wife and dog.
